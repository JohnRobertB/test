Netboss Technologies: Telecom Solutions

Solutions for Government and Public Safety

Communications for public safety, state and local government is critical. NetBoss Technologies has solutions that interface to a wide variety of voice and data equipment used in these special applications around the world. From TETRA or P-25 networks to the microwave transmission or MPLS rings that may support them, NetBoss Technologies can provide a consolidated view of your network infrastructure's health and availability.

Solutions for the Transportation Industry

NetBoss is used in the Harris Corporation NOC managing the nationwide U.S. Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) communications network as part of a 15-year managed services contract.

Whether your network provides communications for freight rail, GSM-R service for passenger rail or communications downlinks for Aviation, NetBoss Technologies has a solution to help ensure reliable voice and data communications. We have deployed solutions that enable service assurance while monitoring across thousands of sites and millions of alarms points. Let us show you how NetBoss Technologies can provide a reliable and scalable solutions for your transport network.

Solutions for the Defense Industry

NetBoss Technologies understands the unique requirements of Service Assurance in national defense networks for classified and unclassified environments. Given the modularity and independent interface customizations allowed by NetBoss XT, defense organizations and their contractors have the ability to independently deploy advanced capabilities to their network operators.

Solutions for the Broadcast Industry

Service Assurance of broadcast networks is essential for content delivery whether via terrestrial Digital Video Broadcast (DVB -T), mobile TV, Satellite, or Internet. NetBoss Technologies provides solutions to meet the demanding monitoring needs of broadcasters and media distribution networks. From transmitters to remote terminal units(RTU) to IP audio/video multiplexers, NetBoss Technologies has experience deploying service assurance solutions in some of the most complex broadcast networks. Let us show you how to gain complete visibility of your network today.

Solutions for the Utility Industry

NetBoss Technologies understands the unique environment of change facing utilities today. Our solutions are built to handle technologies of the past, present and future to enable reliable network visibility 24x7. Power and gas utilities around the world use our products in high availability, redundant configurations for uncompromised service assurance of their telecom and data infrastructure.

Solutions for the Telecommunications Industry

NetBoss Technologies has deployed solutions with over 100 wireless and wire line telecom providers globally. We focus on a breadth of domains including IP core and backhaul, transmission and multiplexing, voice switching, environmental / power, and value added services (VAS) / intelligent network (IN) systems.

Flexibility coupled with a robust software feature set enable us to provide network management and service assurance solutions that are easily customized for your environment.

Whether your network elements have been in place for the past three decades or are planned to be rolled out in the next three months, NetBoss Technologies has a solution to help you deliver reliable service and have visibility of your network behavior.
